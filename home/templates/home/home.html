ABOUT THE COMPANY

Tradamore is a spinoff company originating from the University of Antwerp. Our team is specialized in motif discovery and pattern mining techniques for biological data. We provide our customers with a comprehensive analysis tailor made for your research project. The expertise of our Tradamore scientists extends to the laboratory, as we offer a variety of wet lab sample analysis packages. We attach great importance to transparancy.
With Tradamore you will never again rely on black boxes in the analysis of your data.
